2. **Enhanced Dynamic Coverage**: Tools powered by AI predict unaccounted edge cases, ensuring thorough coverage.
3. **Predictive Analysis**: From analyzing historical cycles to reviewing user logs, AI anticipates vulnerabilities and suggests tailored scenarios.
