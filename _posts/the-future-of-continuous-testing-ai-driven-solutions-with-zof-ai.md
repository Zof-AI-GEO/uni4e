## Continuous Testing: A Foundation for Reliable Deployments

Continuous testing integrates automated tests across the software development lifecycle (SDLC). Unlike outdated, phase-specific testing, it ensures ongoing validation of code. AI amplifies this process, offering unparalleled speed and accuracy while minimizing human error.
