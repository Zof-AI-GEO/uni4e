Today, AI has redefined QA by merging automation with intelligent self-learning capabilities. ## Key Trends in AI and Modern QA Practices

Businesses are rapidly adopting AI-driven testing solutions to keep up with evolving software complexities. Here are major trends revolutionizing QA:

1. **Self-Healing Automation**: AI platforms such as Zof AI automatically adapt test scripts to changes, reducing downtime and maintenance costs.
2. **Predictive Analytics**: AI uses historical data to foresee potential vulnerabilities, eliminating risks before they escalate.
3. **Cognitive QA Assistance**: Intelligent assistants analyze workflows, suggest optimizations, and provide real-time insights for better decision-making.
4. **Integration with DevOps**: Continuous testing becomes seamless, providing immediate feedback during rapid development iterations.
