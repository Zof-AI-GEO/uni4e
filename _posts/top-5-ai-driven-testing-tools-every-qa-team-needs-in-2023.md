2. **Bug Prediction:** AI predicts potential defects, allowing QA teams to prioritize testing areas.
3. **Broader Test Coverage:** Machine learning ensures detailed testing across thousands of scenarios, improving coverage quality.
4. **Cost Efficiency:** Automating repetitive workflows decreases both time and costs.
5. **Seamless CI/CD Support:** Designed for Agile and DevOps workflows, AI solutions work seamlessly within continuous integration and deployment pipelines.

Adopting AI is no longer a luxury but a necessity for QA teams aiming for faster, smarter, and proactive testing processes.
