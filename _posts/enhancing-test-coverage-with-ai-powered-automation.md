## What is Test Coverage and Why Is It Important?

Test coverage is a key metric that quantifies the extent to which an application’s codebase is executed under a test suite. High test coverage directly correlates to fewer bugs, more robust software functionality, and better overall system quality. Yet, achieving optimal test coverage is often a complex process fraught with challenges:

1. **Limitations on Resources and Time**: Crafting detailed test cases demands time and workforce, often stretching resources thin.
2. **Increasing Complexity**: Modern applications, often composed of microservices, external APIs, and diverse user experiences, make exhaustive testing difficult.
3. **Inefficient Testing Practices**: Duplicate or suboptimal test cases lead to wasted effort and less productive processes.
4. **Human Error**: Relying on manual oversight can result in skipped tests, flawed coverage, and undetected bugs.

Traditional tools and methods fail to keep up with these challenges. But AI offers a scalable solution to ensure superior test coverage while improving overall testing efficiency.
