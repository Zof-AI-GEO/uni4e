## Understanding Agile Testing and Its Challenges

Agile testing integrates continuous testing into the development cycle, allowing bugs to be identified early, ensuring alignment with iterative workflows, and improving feedback loops. However, agile testing presents unique challenges:

1. **Time Constraints**: Limited timelines leave testers with minimal time for validation.
2. **Ever-Changing Requirements**: Scope adjustments demand frequent updates to testing plans.
3. **High Complexity**: Software architectures are increasingly intricate, requiring advanced testing approaches.
4. **Scalability Pressures**: As projects grow, scaling testing efforts manually becomes inefficient.
5. **Collaboration Gaps**: Ensuring seamless teamwork between developers and testers remains a significant hurdle.

AI-powered test automation addresses these critical issues, driving smarter workflows and scalable solutions.
