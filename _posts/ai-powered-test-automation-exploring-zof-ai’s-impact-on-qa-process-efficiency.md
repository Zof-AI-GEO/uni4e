What is Test Automation, and Why is AI the Future?

Test automation uses automated tools and scripts to perform software testing, ensuring applications function as intended. It saves time, reduces costs, and offers broader test coverage. However, most traditional test automation techniques rely on static and hard-coded scripts, which often fail to adapt to rapid application changes.

AI enhances this process with dynamic and adaptive methodologies. AI-powered tools like Zof AI bring intelligence and agility to QA by learning from historical data, analyzing patterns, and predicting outcomes. Here’s how AI is redefining test automation:

3. **Speed**: AI runs parallel tests across multiple environments for faster results and fewer development bottlenecks.  
4. **Proactive Vulnerability Detection**: Analyze system behavior, code, and documentation to pinpoint areas prone to bugs before they occur.
