Challenges and Opportunities of AI in QA

### **Challenges:**
- **Data Requirements:** Lack of quality data affects AI algorithm precision.
- **Bias Risks:** Skewed training data may impact outcomes.
- **Compatibility:** Integrating AI into legacy systems remains challenging.

### **Opportunities:**
- **Efficiency Gains:** Streamlined automation saves time and resources.
- **Proactive Risk Mitigation:** Predicting errors before deployment enhances reliability.
- **Scalability:** Machine learning adapts to enterprise-level testing complexities seamlessly.
