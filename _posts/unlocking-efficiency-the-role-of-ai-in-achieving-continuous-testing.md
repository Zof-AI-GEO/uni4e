## Understanding Continuous Testing and Why It’s Essential

Continuous testing ensures automated validation of code changes throughout the software development lifecycle. Unlike conventional testing methods that wait until development concludes, continuous testing operates during development itself, providing real-time feedback for rapid iterations.

### Why Continuous Testing Matters:
- **Faster development cycles:** Automated testing accelerates validation processes.
- **Reduced bugs:** Early detection avoids costly issues during later stages.
- **Operational assurance:** Teams confidently ensure quality without hindering efficiency.

The challenge, however, lies in creating scalable workflows, where AI serves as a transformative solution by revolutionizing test automation, eliminating redundancies, and refining testing accuracy.
