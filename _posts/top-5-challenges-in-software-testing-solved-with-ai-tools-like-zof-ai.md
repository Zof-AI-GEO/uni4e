### 1. Test Case Creation and Prioritization
Manually writing exhaustive test cases is time-intensive, and prioritizing critical scenarios becomes increasingly challenging as applications scale.

### 2. Bug Detection and Regression Testing
Tracking hidden defects and ensuring new updates don’t disrupt existing features pose significant challenges to QA teams.

### 3. Extended Test Execution Timelines
As testing scripts and configurations multiply, execution time grows, creating bottlenecks and management obstacles.

### 4. Agile & DevOps Compatibility
Rapid iterations in Agile and CI/CD pipelines often leave little time for conducting exhaustive, accurate testing.

### 5. Inefficient Resource Utilization
Repetitive manual tasks drain resources, reduce productivity, and increase the risk of oversight.
