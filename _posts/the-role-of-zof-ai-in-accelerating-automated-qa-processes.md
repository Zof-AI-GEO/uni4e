## The Evolution of Automated QA and Limitations

Automated QA utilizes testing scripts and tools like Selenium, JUnit, and TestNG to run recurring tests and uncover bugs, saving significant time compared to manual methods. Yet, it often lacks contextual understanding, generates false positives/negatives, requires tedious script updates, struggles with scalability, and still needs human testers for certain exploratory tasks. To bridge these gaps, AI tools unlock deeper, contextual insights and process adaptability.
