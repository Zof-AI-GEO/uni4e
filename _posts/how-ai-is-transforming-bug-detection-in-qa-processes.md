## The Evolution of Bug Detection

Historically, QA relied on manual testing, rule-based systems, and static automation tools. However, these methods struggled with scalability and identifying unknown issues. AI flips the script by enabling:

- **Machine Learning Algorithms** to recognize bug patterns.
- **Dynamic Adaptability** to detect unforeseen errors.
- **NLP Tools** analyzing user feedback for hidden bugs.
- **Test Case Optimization** reducing redundancies.
- **Proactive Real-Time Monitoring** identifying errors during runtime.
