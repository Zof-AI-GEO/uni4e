## what is predictive testing?

Predictive testing leverages AI to anticipate and address testing challenges before they arise. By analyzing historical data and detecting patterns, AI tools forecast potential system failures, inefficiencies, and bottlenecks that may disrupt performance. Unlike traditional testing, AI-enabled predictive testing mimics human reasoning while managing complex datasets. This empowers development teams to identify bugs preemptively, reduce testing redundancies, and focus attention on high-priority areas, guaranteeing a high-quality product.
